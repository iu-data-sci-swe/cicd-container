# Exercise 2: End-of-Quarter Exams Project Plan

## Project Overview
**Project:** End-of-Quarter Exams  
**Duration:** 6 weeks  
**Objective:** Successfully deliver end-of-quarter examinations with high quality and fairness

---

## Milestones and Deliverables

### Milestone 1: Exam Planning and Design (Week 1-2)
**Deliverables:**
- Exam syllabus coverage matrix
- Question bank with difficulty ratings
- Exam blueprint (topic distribution, time allocation)
- Review rubric and grading criteria
- Accessibility accommodations plan

**Dependencies:** None  
**Stakeholders:** Faculty, Department Head, Disability Services

---

### Milestone 2: Exam Material Development (Week 3-4)
**Deliverables:**
- Draft exam questions (multiple versions if needed)
- Answer keys and marking schemes
- Peer review feedback incorporated
- Sample solutions and partial credit guidelines
- Exam instructions document

**Dependencies:** Milestone 1  
**Stakeholders:** Faculty, Peer Reviewers, Teaching Assistants

---

### Milestone 3: Logistics and Infrastructure (Week 4-5)
**Deliverables:**
- Exam venue bookings confirmed
- Invigilator schedules finalized
- Student seating arrangements
- Exam materials printed/prepared
- Technical setup verified (if computer-based)
- Emergency procedures documented

**Dependencies:** Milestone 2  
**Stakeholders:** Administrative Staff, IT Support, Facilities Management

---

### Milestone 4: Exam Administration (Week 5)
**Deliverables:**
- Exams conducted as scheduled
- Incident reports (if any)
- Attendance records
- Collected answer sheets
- Special accommodation completion logs

**Dependencies:** Milestone 3  
**Stakeholders:** Faculty, Invigilators, Students, Disability Services

---

### Milestone 5: Grading and Review (Week 5-6)
**Deliverables:**
- All exams graded
- Grade normalization analysis
- Quality assurance review completed
- Student review/appeal process established
- Grade dispute resolutions

**Dependencies:** Milestone 4  
**Stakeholders:** Faculty, Teaching Assistants, Department Head

---

### Milestone 6: Results Publication and Feedback (Week 6)
**Deliverables:**
- Final grades published
- Student feedback forms
- Exam statistics and analysis report
- Recommendations for next quarter
- Archive of exam materials

**Dependencies:** Milestone 5  
**Stakeholders:** Faculty, Students, Registrar's Office, Academic Affairs

---

## Risk Management

| Risk | Impact | Mitigation |
|------|--------|------------|
| Exam paper leak | High | Multiple versions, secure storage |
| Technical failures | Medium | Backup systems, contingency plans |
| Illness outbreak | Medium | Makeup exam procedures |
| Grading inconsistencies | Medium | Inter-rater reliability checks |
| Student appeals | Low | Clear rubrics, transparent process |

---

## Success Criteria
- Exams completed on schedule
- 95%+ student participation rate
- <5% grade appeals
- Positive student feedback (>70% satisfaction)
- Fair distribution of grades matching course performance
